Our Heritage & Story

Three decades of preserving India's artistic legacy

Established 1985

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Blueblood Exports was founded to bring India's extraordinary artistic heritage to the global stage while ensuring prosperity for artisan communities.

What began as a small family enterprise has grown into one of India's most respected exporters. We work with over 200 artisan families across 15 states.

Our Artisan Communities

Celebrating the hands that create our masterpieces

Brass Artisans of Tamil Nadu

The Sthapathis of Swamimalai have crafted bronze idols for over 800 years using the lost-wax technique.

Woodcarvers of Rajasthan

From Jodhpur, our woodcarvers transform native sheesham into intricate furniture reflecting Rajput artistry.

Weavers of Varanasi

Master weavers continue a 3000-year tradition, handlooming silk tapestries using ancient techniques.
