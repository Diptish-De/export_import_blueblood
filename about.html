Our Heritage & Story

Three decades of preserving India's artistic legacy

Established 2026

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Blueblood Exports was founded to bring India's extraordinary artistic heritage to the global stage while ensuring prosperity for artisan communities.

What began as a small family enterprise has grown into one of India's most respected exporters. We work with over 200 artisan families across 15 states.

Our Artisan Network

Preserving the Soul of Indian Artistry

At Blueblood Exports, we believe every artefact carries the spirit of its maker. We work directly with over 200 artisan families across India, ensuring fair wages and the preservation of techniques that define our civilizational identity. From ancient lost-wax casting to intricate bone inlay, we don't just export products; we export legacy.
