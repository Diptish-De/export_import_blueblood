How It Works

A seamless journey from inquiry to delivery

1

Submit Inquiry

Browse our catalogue, select products, and send an inquiry via WhatsApp with your requirements.

2

Quote & Confirmation

3

Production & QC

Artisans craft your order. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality checks before packaging.

4

Secure Packaging

Export-grade packaging with protective materials ensures safe transit across continents.

5

Shipping & Tracking

Shipped via trusted carriers with full documentation. Track your shipment in real-time.

6

Delivery & Support

Receive your artefacts with all certificates. Our team is available for any post-delivery queries.

Minimum Orders

MOQ varies by product (typically 5-50 units). We accommodate smaller orders for samples at adjusted pricing.

Quality Checks

Multi-stage inspection at artisan workshop, our facility, and before final packaging ensures zero defects.

Delivery Timeline

Standard orders ship within 2-4 weeks. Express options available. Customs clearance support provided.
